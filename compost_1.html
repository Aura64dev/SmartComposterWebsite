Description

A problem with current composting methods is the signifigant amount of both time and space they require in order to remain effective. The Smart Composter is a product that attempts to eliminate the difficulties presented by composting if one has time or space constraints that hamper their ability to have a well-maintained compost pile.

Background research

There are many factors to consider when creating a project such as this, especially the science behind the steps undergone by people trying to tend to their compost by hand. There is also necessary market research to determine whether or not a project like this already exists and whether or not there is a customer base for it.
