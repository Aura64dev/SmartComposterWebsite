Design

Construction

The Smart Composter is made of 3d printed materials and wood. It uses pH, temperature, and humidity sensors to determine when to add leaves into the composter and Peltier tiles to generate heat which helps the decomposition process. To assist in temperature regulation the Peltier tiles are covered in a layer of tin foil insulation to trap the heat in the compost. To keep humidity at a consistent percentage we have implemented an automatic ventilation system. At the center of the unit is the stepper motor which spins and stirs the compost for even distribution of newly-added leaves as well as regulation of the moisture level of the soil. The LCD provides instant feedback to the user by displaying the current readings of temperature, leaf amount, and humidity.
